This is a mathematical game that tests your fast ability to calculate numbers with your mind. You begin with 3 happy faces and are only introduced to 3 levels.
Level 1 deals with multiplying numbers of one digit,
Lefel 2 deals with two digits,
Level 3 deals with three digits,
If all smiley faces are striked with a red cross, you're out of the game.
You are set with a timer:
Level: 1 - 10 seconds
Level: 2 - 20 seconds
Level: 3 - 30 seconds
